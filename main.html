About Wohaib Khalid

A seasoned Management Consultant based in Doha, Qatar, with a passion for driving strategic growth across the sports, business, and entrepreneurial sectors. My work focuses on mentoring the next generation of leaders and analyzing the economic impact of mega-events.

With deep expertise in business strategy, market analysis, and financial modeling, I help organizations navigate complexity and achieve their goals. I am committed to fostering innovation and guiding ventures from ideation to successful execution within Qatar's dynamic startup ecosystem.
